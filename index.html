// 02 — The Problem
THE
PROBLEM
WE SOLVE
01

Industry Mismatch

The creative technology sector is rapidly expanding into automotive, AR/VR, robotics, and smart devices — yet our current program trains primarily for film and entertainment pipelines.

02

Tool Stack Gap

Graduates leave with Maya, Photoshop and After Effects — strong foundations, but missing Unity, TouchDesigner, Arduino, Raspberry Pi and generative AI tools now standard in studios.

03

Missed Revenue

Working professionals in the region have no structured upskilling pathway. CPD courses and industry training remain an untapped revenue stream for the university.

// 03 — Program Vision
To train the next generation of creative technologists who design at the intersection of animation, code, hardware, and human experience
A 4-year degree bridging creative disciplines with emerging technology — producing graduates ready for automotive, XR, AI, robotics, and interactive media industries.
